About me

Moved from Germany to Japan in Autumn 2000.

Working in a largish enterprise environment in system engineering (software).

In private life I prefer the smaller side: Arduino, Orange Pi, wireless routers, Bluetooth, also, or maybe even more, using cloud computing (AWS, GC), Docker and all the software development practices like CI/CD, Unit Testing, TDD etc.

The magic is to combine those two totally different worlds and find...I can't find a better word than "synergies", and there are surprisingly many.
